🔥 Our Journey: Rising From The Ashes

As many of our loyal customers know, the recent fire at Gul Plaza was a devastating moment for us. Our physical shop was completely destroyed, but while the walls are gone, our spirit and your trust remain untouched.

This tragedy hasn't stopped us—it has only fueled our mission to bring joy to your children through our online store.

🚀 Our New Chapter

Losing our physical home at Gul Plaza was a turning point. We decided that the smiles of our little friends shouldn't fade because of a fire.

We have now shifted our focus entirely to our digital platform to ensure that no matter where you are in Pakistan, you can still access the premium toys you love.

🛡️ Safety First, Always

We understand that as parents, your child’s safety is your top priority.

That is why we hand-pick every single toy in our collection. From BPA-free plastics to non-toxic paints, we ensure that every item we sell meets the highest safety standards before it ever reaches your doorstep.

🧸 Curated For Every Child

Whether it’s high-speed RC cars for little racers, educational blocks for budding engineers, or beautiful dolls for imaginative play—our collection is vast and varied.

We source unique and high-quality toys from around the globe to ensure your child always has something new and exciting to learn from.

✨ The WsToys Promise

"Our shop may have burned down, but our commitment to quality is stronger than ever."

We continue to sell the best and most premium toys that bring families together.

Every order you place helps us rebuild and continue our journey of spreading happiness.
